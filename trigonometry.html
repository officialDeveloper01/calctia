Key Concepts and Formulas

  • Sign Functions
    • sin(-θ) = -sinθ
    • cos(-θ) = cosθ
    • tan(-θ) = -tanθ
    • cosec(-θ) = -cosecθ
    • sec(-θ) = secθ
    • cot(-θ) = -cotθ
  • Identities
    • sin²A + cos²A = 1
    • tan²A + 1 = sec²A
    • cot²A + 1 = cosec²A
