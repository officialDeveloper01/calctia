Key Concepts and Formulas

  • Sign Functions
    • sin(-θ) = -sinθ
    • cos(-θ) = cosθ
    • tan(-θ) = -tanθ
    • cosec(-θ) = -cosecθ
    • sec(-θ) = secθ
    • cot(-θ) = -cotθ
  • Identities
    • sin2A + cos2A = 1
    • tan2A + 1 = sec2A
    • cot2A + 1 = cosec2A
