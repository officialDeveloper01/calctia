Introduction to Statistics

Statistics is a branch of mathematics dealing with data collection, organization, analysis, and interpretation. It helps in making informed decisions based on numerical data.

YouTube Tutorial

Key Statistical Concepts and Formulas

  • Mean (Average): (Sum of all values) / (Number of values)
  • Median: The middle value when data is arranged in order
  • Mode: The value that appears most frequently
  • Standard Deviation: A measure of data dispersion
